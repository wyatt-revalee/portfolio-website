About Me

I am currently a student enrolled at Indiana State Unversity, pursuing a Bachelors Degree in Computer Science, with a concentration on computing science.
Right now I am finishing up my degree and will be graduating in May of 2023.

Throughout my time at university, I have ed on many things ranging from simple programs from school, to big projects like creating a game using Unity and C#. Feel free to take a look at the projects I've worked on in the Works section.

My hope is to be able to make an impact on the world through software and coding, while building all the skills I can along the way.

My interest in computer science and technology in general started when I was young. Technology was all around me and as I grew up, it did too. I was able to experience that in real time, and I realized I wanted to be a part of it.
